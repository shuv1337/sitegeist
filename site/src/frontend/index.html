We Don't Want Your Data

Use Your Browser

Works in Chrome, Edge, Brave, and other Chromium-based browsers. No custom browser required, no data mining.

Your Choice of AI

Use OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, OpenRouter, Groq, Cerebras, xAI, Z.ai, or self-hosted and local models via Ollama, LM Studio, llama.cpp, vLLM, SGLM.

Privacy First

Your settings, API keys, and chat history stay on your computer. Only your messages go to your chosen AI provider—configured to not retain or train on your data.

Your Data, Your Control

Export your entire chat history and settings at any time. Import them anywhere. No lock-in, no vendor control.

Skills, Artifacts, and Attachments

Skills

Teach Sitegeist how to interact with any website once, save it as a "skill," and reuse it forever. Skills are site-specific automation libraries that load automatically when you visit matching domains.

Example: Create a skill for your favorite store's search and product extraction. Next time you visit, Sitegeist already knows how to navigate and scrape.

Artifacts

Living documents that persist throughout your session. Create markdown notes, interactive HTML dashboards, images, PDFs, or Excel files. Update them as you work, and download when done.

Example: Start a research note when investigating a topic. As Sitegeist navigates and extracts data, it continuously updates your note with findings and sources.

Attachments

Drop files right into the chat. PDFs, Office documents, images—Sitegeist reads them all. Ask questions about uploaded files, extract data, or combine information across multiple documents.

FAQ

How much does Sitegeist cost?

Sitegeist will be a paid service with a monthly subscription in the $5-10 range. This includes access to the latest updates and maintained skills—libraries and prompts for popular websites that need constant updates as sites change. You can also create your own skills. You bring your own API keys for LLM providers or use free, open-weights local models at no charge. This pricing model allows us to keep working on Sitegeist without the wrong incentives.

Do I need an account?

Yes. You'll sign up with sitegeist.ai for license enforcement. We only store your payment info and license information—nothing else.

What browsers are supported?

Desktop Chromium browsers: Chrome, Edge, Brave, and others. Firefox support is coming soon—we're waiting for them to implement the userScripts.execute() API.

Can I use local models?

Yes. Any OpenAI Chat Completions or Responses compatible endpoint works—Ollama, LM Studio, llama.cpp, vLLM, SGLM, and more.

What data is stored and sent?

Everything stays local—your settings, API keys, and chat history. Only your chat messages go to your chosen LLM provider, configured to not retain or train on your data. Sitegeist will also make requests to sitegeist.ai to check that you have a valid license.

Is Sitegeist open source?

No, Sitegeist is source available. As a Chrome extension, we must package all code in non-obfuscated form for Web Store reviewers. This means you (or a technical friend) can dissect it too. While Sitegeist is unlikely to become truly open-source, we ensure the sources we ship make it easy to review what Sitegeist is doing. You can also observe any and all network requests it performs.
